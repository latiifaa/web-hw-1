super mario galaxy & odyssey

It's an adventure game of Mario explores different kingdoms and collect Power Moons hidden inside them to save the princess

  1. The game begins with the story of the kidnapping of the princess
  2. Mario starts traveling from one kingdom to another
  3. He starts to collects the Power Moons
  4. When he reaches a certain number of Power Moons, he can defeats the villain
  5. Finally, the princess goes back to her palace
